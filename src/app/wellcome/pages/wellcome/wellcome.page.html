What is e-Secrets?

e-Secrets is a secrets manger that aims to keep all your electronic private information, in a simple and easy way.

How it works?

How it works?

All your data is stored locally on your device in an electronic vault that is sealed using the most advanced and updated encryption technologies (RSA and AES256). This vault can only be unsealed by your master password, or using the biomettric verification methods provided by your device.
