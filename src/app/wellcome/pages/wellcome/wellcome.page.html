What is e-Secrets?

e-Secrets is a secrets manger that aims to manage all your electronic private information, in a easy and simple way.

How it works?

How it works?

With only one password e-Secrets keep and manage all your other passwords or private information. You can register and keep information like: passwords, login credentials like email, Fakebook, Instagran, and others, and also bank account, cred card, and many others.

Is it safe?

e-Secrets is safe because

All your information is kept locally on your device and sealed using the most advanced and updated encryption technologies (RSA and AES-256), that can only be unsealed by a master password, defined by you, or via biometric verification (if your device supports).

You don't need to register any account, only the password to encrypt your data. We offer to backup and sync your data on some cloud storage service like Google Drive or Dropbox. However, in this case your data will left your device already sealed and so it will be kept in the cloud. There is no way it can be violated, unless with the passes of the master password, so keep it safe.
